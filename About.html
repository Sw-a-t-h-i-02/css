About Cafe Bahar

Cafe Bahar is a hyderabadi restaurant, chinese restaurant, indian restaurant, kebab shop and mughlai restaurant located in Hyderabad, Telangana. The average rating of this place is 4.20 out of 5 stars based on 45808 reviews. The street address of this place is 3-5, 815/A, Old MLA Quarters Rd, Avanti Nagar, Himayatnagar, Hyderabad, Telangana 500029, India. It is about 0.39 kilometers away from the Himayatnagar railway station.
